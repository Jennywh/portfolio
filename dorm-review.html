Github Repo

Don't we all want to hear other people's opinions of the on-campus dorms as freshmen? I remember the difficulties that I had when trying to select a dorm out of 36 dorms. It could have been an easier choice for the freshmen. So I developed a site for students to leave reviews and comments of dorms they've lived in. These opinions will be very helpful for incoming students.

I also implemented the ArcGIS map to display the exact location of the dorm. Average ratings are shown when clicked on. It helps students pick the highest-rated dorm within the range of area they would live to reside in.
