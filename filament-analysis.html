Filament Analysis

Neuroscience Research | Matlab | Data Structure | Imaris

AutoHotKey Github Repo

I worked in the Reissner Lab at the UNC Department of Psychology and Neuroscience. I programmed in Matlab to track the nodes of the astrocyte filaments. I also categorized them by their properties, and output their data in a format. I also automated Imaris to run hundreds of astrocytes through AutoHotKey scripts.












A sample astrocyte filament displayed (in green) in Imaris 3D view, with branching nodes marked in white dots.
